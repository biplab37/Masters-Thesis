    Let's focus on the second order correction in $V_1$ to two point function. This gives integral of the form
    \begin{equation}
        V^1_2\int^{\infty}_{-\infty}\frac{d\omega_1d\omega_2}{(2\pi)^2}\int_{\frac{\Lambda}{s}<|k_1|,|k_2|<\Lambda}\frac{d^2k_1d^2k_2}{(2\pi)^4} G^{(0)}_1(i\omega_1,k_1)G^{(0)}_1(i\omega_2,k_2)G^{(0)}_1(-i(\omega_1 + \omega_2),k-k_1-k_2)
    \end{equation}
    where we have used the momentum conservation at each vertices to substitute for $k_3$ and $\omega_3$.

    At first we can look at one of the $\omega$ integrals
    \begin{equation}
        \sum_{s,s'=\pm 1}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \frac{d\omega_1}{2\pi}\frac{1 + s \frac{\sigma . k_1}{|k_1|}}{i\omega_1 - sv_F|k_1|} \frac{1 + s'\frac{\sigma . (k-k_1-k_2)}{|k-k_1-k_2|}}{i(\omega_1 + \omega_2) - s'v_F|k-k_1-k_2|}
    \end{equation}
    Poles are at $\pm v_F|k_1|$ and at $-i\omega_2 \pm v_F|k-k_1-k_2|$. We can close the contour on either side to get residues from only two of them. This gives the integral to be
    \begin{equation}
        \left(1 + \tfrac{\sigma . k_1}{|k_1|}\right)\sum_{s'=\pm 1} \tfrac{1 + s'\tfrac{\sigma . (k-k_1-k_2)}{|k-k_1-k_2|}}{i\omega_2 + v_F|k_1| - s'v_F|k-k_1-k_2|} + \left(1 + \tfrac{\sigma . (k-k_1-k_2)}{|k-k_1-k_2|}\right)\sum_{s = \pm 1}\tfrac{1 + s\frac{\sigma . k_1}{|k_1|}}{-i\omega_2 + v_F|k-k_1-k_2| - sv_F|k_1|}
    \end{equation}
    Note that the terms with $s = s' = 1$ cancels each other. For the other $\omega$ integral we first have to multiply the above result by $G_1^{(0)}(i\omega_2,k_2)$. All the terms in the integrands are of the form 
    \begin{equation}
        \int_{\infty}^{\infty}\frac{d\omega_2}{2\pi}\frac{1}{(i\omega_2 - a)(i\omega_2 -b)}
    \end{equation}
    where a and b are real numbers. The integrals trivially reduces to $\frac{\theta(b) - \theta(a)}{a-b}$. Thus we only have non zero contribution if a and b are of different signs. In this case a can take values from $\pm v_F|k_2|$ and b can take values from $\pm v_F(|k_1| + |k-k_1-k_2|)$. Closing the contour such a way to take only the contribution from the positive ones we get,
    \begin{equation}
        \left(1 + \hat{\sigma}_{k_1}\right)\left(1 + \hat{\sigma}_{k_2}\right)\left(1 - \hat{\sigma}_{k-k_1-k_2}\right)\tfrac{1}{v_F(|k_1|+|k_2| + |k-k_2-k_2|)} + \left(1 + \hat{\sigma}_{k-k_1-k_2}\right)\left(1 - \hat{\sigma}_{k_1}\right)\tfrac{1}{v_F(|k_1|-|k_2|+|k-k_1-k_2|)}
    \end{equation}
    where $\hat{\sigma}_{k} = \frac{\sigma . k}{|k|}$.
